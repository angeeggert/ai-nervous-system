## ðŸ’¡ Troubleshooting & Pro-Tips

*   **"The AI is asking for a file I can't find."**
    *   *Cause:* You likely renamed the file on your disk but didn't update the path in the Index.
    *   *Fix:* Use your OS search for keywords. Once found, update the Index to match the real filename.

*   **"The Astrolabe is repeating old entries."**
    *   *Cause:* Context pollution. The AI remembers the previous batch too strongly.
    *   *Fix:* Use a fresh chat window for every major batch (e.g., every 10 files).

*   **"The Index is getting too big for the chat window."**
    *   *Fix:* You have reached "Data Saturation". Use the **Fulgurit Generator** (from the Gardener's Handbook) to compress old logs into summaries, then delete the raw logs from the Index. Keep the Index lean.
